Instructions

Welcome to Stock Searcher! To use this program, first navigate to the "search" tab at the top of the screen. Next, input a ticker symbol (stock symbol) or the name of any company to get information about the stock such as links, live price data, a description, and more. To add or remove a stock from the your personalized list of favorites just hit the heart; When the heart is read it can be found under favorites, and if it is white it is removed. If you want to find stocks to search, you can click the "top tickers" buttons under the searchbar in the "search" page to display the ticker symbols of some of the largest companies today, or check out the index under the Stock Index tab for hundreds of the largest companies in the country.

Instructions

Welcome to Stock Searcher! To use this program, first navigate to the "search" tab at the top of the screen. Next, input a ticker symbol (stock symbol) or the name of any companyto get information about the stock such as links, live price data, a description, and more. To add or delete a stock from the your personalized list of favorites just hit the heart so that when it is red is it a favortie and when it is white it is not. If you want to find stocks to search, you can click the "top tickers" buttons under the searchbar in the "search" page to display the ticker symbols of some of the largest companies today.

Welcome to Stock Searcher! To use this program, first navigate to the "search" tab at the top of the screen. Next, input a ticker symbol (stock symbol) of any company's stock to get information about the stock such as links, live price data, a description, and more. If you want to find stocks to search, you can click the "top tickers" buttons under the searchbar in the "search" page to display the ticker symbols of some of the largest companies today.
